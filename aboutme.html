Archivist for Audio Materials

Who I Am

Hello! I'm a passionate Archivist for Audio Materials at AudioLibs. I specialize in preserving sound history and curating valuable audio resources for the community.

My Mission

To safeguard and promote access to the audio heritage of our community, ensuring that voices, sounds, and stories are preserved for present and future generations.

My Vision

To build a living archive where sound materials connect people across time, culture, and experiences through the magic of audio.

Objectives

  • Collect and archive community-based audio materials.
  • Promote the importance of preserving sound culture.
  • Engage the community through audio exhibitions and workshops.
  • Ensure digital access and long-term preservation of audio collections.

Achievements

  • Archived over 300 hours of local oral histories and podcasts.
  • Developed a digital repository for public access.
  • Organized the "Voices of the Community" exhibition in 2024.
  • Collaborated with cultural institutions for audio heritage education.
