Frequently Asked Questions

What is AudioLibs?
AudioLibs is a digital sound library dedicated to preserving, archiving, and providing access to audio-based knowledge and stories.
How can I access the audio catalog?
You can explore our full audio catalog by visiting the "Catalog" page in the navigation bar. All items are available for listening and download where applicable.
Can I contribute my own audio materials?
Yes! We encourage community contributions. Please contact us through the "Contact" page to submit or donate audio materials.
Are the audiobooks free to use?
Yes, all our audiobooks are in the public domain or under open licenses and are available free of charge.
How do I contact the archivist?
You can use the form on the "Contact" page or email us directly for inquiries about services or collaboration opportunities.
